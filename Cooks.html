Chicago-style Italian Beef

Chicago-style Italian Beef

Serves 12 Quality - Excellent

Ingredients

  • 1 Qt. Beef Stock
  • 1 qt. Water
  • 3 oz. tomato paste
  • 2 ts. Pepper, dried red crushed
  • 2 ts. Salt (because of the salt in the stock, usually not added)
  • 4 bay leaves
  • 4 lb. Beef,Rare roast, sliced thin
  • 4 peppers, green, sliced

Preparation

  1. Mix together.
  2. Beef could be used from the deli or for best results, roast your own beef.
  3. Cover it with Italian spices then only bake until very rare.
  4. Let it cool before slicing.
  5. Works best in a crock-pot and let to simmer over several hours.
  6. Serve in Italian bread buns.
  7. Also great with broth soaked into the rolls.
  8. Additional sweet or hot peppers may be added to taste.
